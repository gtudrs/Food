Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp (5 g) dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  • Step 1: Wash and prepare the vegetables. Chop the tomatoes into bite-sized pieces, slice the cucumber into thin rounds, and cut the red onion into fine slices.
  • Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, cucumber slices, red onion, and Kalamata olives. Toss them gently to distribute evenly.
  • Step 3: Crumble the feta cheese over the salad, making sure it is evenly spread for a balanced flavor in each bite.
  • Step 4: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. This creates the dressing that enhances the fresh ingredients.
  • Step 5: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss everything together to coat the ingredients evenly.
  • Step 6: Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together before serving.
  • Step 7: Serve fresh and enjoy alongside grilled meats, pita bread, or on its own as a light and refreshing dish.
