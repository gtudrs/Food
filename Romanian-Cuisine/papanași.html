Papanași

Ingredients:

  • 250 g fresh cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 50 g sugar
  • 150 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) baking soda
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 200 g sour cream
  • 100 g fruit jam (cherry, blueberry, or raspberry)

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cottage cheese, egg, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well until smooth.
  • Step 2: Gradually add the flour and baking soda, kneading gently until a soft but non-sticky dough forms.
  • Step 3: Divide the dough into small portions. Shape each into a round donut shape, leaving a hole in the middle.
  • Step 4: Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 175°C (350°F). Fry the doughnuts in batches for about 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  • Step 5: Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Step 6: Serve warm, topped with sour cream and a generous spoonful of fruit jam.
  • Step 7: Enjoy this traditional Romanian dessert with a dusting of powdered sugar if desired!
