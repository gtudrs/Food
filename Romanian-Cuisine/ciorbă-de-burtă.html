Ciorbă de Burtă

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef tripe, cleaned and cut into strips
  • 2 L beef broth
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 200 ml sour cream
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large pot, bring the beef broth to a boil. Add the cleaned tripe and cook for about 1.5 to 2 hours until tender.
  • Step 2: While the tripe is cooking, sauté the grated carrot and chopped onion in a pan with a little oil until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Step 3: Remove the tripe from the broth and cut it into thin strips if needed. Return it to the pot along with the sautéed vegetables.
  • Step 4: In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sour cream, and vinegar. Slowly add a ladle of hot broth to the mixture, stirring continuously to prevent curdling.
  • Step 5: Gradually pour the egg mixture back into the soup, stirring constantly. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes without boiling to maintain a creamy consistency.
  • Step 6: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and black pepper. Stir well and let the flavors blend.
  • Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley, and enjoy with a side of sour cream and chili peppers.
