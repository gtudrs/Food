Ciorbă de Perișoare

Ingredients:

  • 500 g ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 50 g rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1.5 L beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) tomato paste
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large pot, heat some oil and sauté the onions, carrots, celery, and bell pepper for 5 minutes until softened.
  • Step 2: Add the diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Stir well and let it cook for another 5 minutes to develop the flavor.
  • Step 3: Pour in the broth and bring it to a boil. Let it simmer while preparing the meatballs.
  • Step 4: In a bowl, mix the ground pork, egg, rice, salt, and black pepper. Knead well until the mixture is uniform.
  • Step 5: Form small meatballs and carefully drop them into the simmering soup.
  • Step 6: Cook for about 25-30 minutes until the meatballs are fully cooked and tender.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm with a side of sour cream and crusty bread.
