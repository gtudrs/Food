Roasted Turkey

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey (4-6 kg)
  • 100 g butter (softened)
  • 3 tbsp (45 ml) olive oil
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) salt
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) black pepperent
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) onion powder
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) dried rosemary
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) dried sage
  • 1 lemon (halved)
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 4 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 500 ml chicken or turkey broth (for basting)

Steps:

  • Step 1: Prepare the Turkey: Remove the turkey from the fridge 1 hour before cooking to bring it to room temperature. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Pat the turkey dry with paper towels and remove any giblets.
  • Step 2: Season the Turkey: In a bowl, mix softened butter, olive oil, salt, pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, rosemary, and sage. Gently loosen the skin of the turkey and rub half of the butter mixture under the skin. Rub the remaining butter mixture all over the turkey’s surface.
  • Step 3: Stuff the Turkey (Optional): Place lemon halves, onion quarters, and crushed garlic inside the turkey cavity for extra flavor.
  • Step 4: Roasting the Turkey: Place the turkey breast-side up on a rack in a roasting pan. Pour 500 ml of broth into the pan to keep the turkey moist. Cover loosely with aluminum foil and roast for 45 minutes per kg (e.g., a 5 kg turkey = about 3 hours 45 minutes).
  • Step 5: Basting the Turkey: Every 45 minutes, baste the turkey with the pan juices. Remove the foil for the last 45 minutes to allow the skin to become golden and crispy.
  • Step 6: The turkey is done when the internal temperature reaches 75°C (check at the thickest part of the breast and thigh using a meat thermometer). If you don’t have a thermometer, pierce the thickest part of the thigh—if the juices run clear, it's done.
  • Step 7: Rest the Turkey: Remove the turkey from the oven and let it rest for 30 minutes before carving. This helps retain the juices.
  • Step 8: Serve: Slice and serve with gravy, mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or your favorite sides.
