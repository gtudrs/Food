Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp (30 g) butter
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 100 g smoked bacon (diced)
  • 2 medium potatoes (about 300 g, diced)
  • 500 g sweet corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 L chicken or vegetable broth
  • 250 ml heavy cream (or whole milk)
  • 1 tsp (5 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp (10 g) cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp (30 ml) water (optional for thickening)
  • Fresh parsley or chives for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Cook the Bacon: Heat butter and olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add diced bacon and cook until crispy (about 5 minutes). Remove the bacon with a slotted spoon and set aside (leave the fat in the pot).
  • Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics: In the same pot, add onion and cook for 3 minutes until soft. Add garlic, smoked paprika, and thyme, stirring for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the Potatoes and Corn: Stir in potatoes and corn, cooking for 2 minutes.
  • Step 4: Simmer the Soup: Pour in chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes until potatoes are tender.
  • Step 5: Blend (Optional for Creamy Texture): Use an immersion blender to partially blend the soup for a thicker consistency. If you prefer chunky chowder, skip this step.
  • Step 6: Finish with Cream & Bacon: Stir in heavy cream and the cooked bacon. Simmer for 5 more minutes. If the soup is too thin, mix cornstarch with water, then stir it into the pot and cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Step 7: Serve & Garnish: Ladle into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or chives. Serve hot with crusty bread or crackers.
