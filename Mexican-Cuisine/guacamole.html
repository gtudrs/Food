Guacamole

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper

Steps:

  • Step 1: Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  • Step 2: Mash the avocado with a fork until you reach your desired consistency – smooth or chunky.
  • Step 3: Add the chopped onion, tomato, garlic, and jalapeño (if using). Stir well to combine.
  • Step 4: Squeeze in the lime juice and mix thoroughly to enhance the flavor and prevent browning.
  • Step 5: Stir in the chopped cilantro, salt, and black pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  • Step 6: Cover with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the guacamole to prevent oxidation.
  • Step 7: Serve fresh with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a topping for your favorite dishes!
