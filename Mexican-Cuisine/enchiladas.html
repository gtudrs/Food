Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 cups (300 g) cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup (200 g) shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • 1 can (400 g) enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) sour cream
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil
  • 1 tsp (5 g) ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) chili powder
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly grease a baking dish to prevent sticking.
  • Step 2: In a pan over medium heat, warm the olive oil and sauté the chopped onions until soft and translucent, about 3 minutes.
  • Step 3: Add the shredded chicken, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes to blend the flavors.
  • Step 4: Warm the tortillas slightly to make them pliable. Fill each tortilla with the chicken mixture and some shredded cheese, then roll them tightly.
  • Step 5: Place the rolled tortillas seam-side down in the baking dish. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the top, ensuring each one is well coated.
  • Step 6: Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the enchiladas and bake for 20 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Step 7: Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly. Garnish with sour cream and fresh cilantro before serving.
