Salmorejo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 150 g stale bread, torn into pieces
  • 100 ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) red wine vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 boiled eggs, chopped (for garnish)
  • 100 g cured ham (Jamón Serrano), diced (for garnish)

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a blender, add the chopped tomatoes and blend until smooth. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to remove seeds and skin.
  • Step 2: Add the torn bread to the tomato mixture and let it soak for 10 minutes to soften.
  • Step 3: Add the minced garlic, red wine vinegar, and a pinch of salt. Blend again until well combined.
  • Step 4: Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while blending to achieve a creamy consistency.
  • Step 5: Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to let the flavors meld.
  • Step 6: Serve chilled, garnished with chopped boiled eggs and diced Jamón Serrano.
  • Step 7: Enjoy this refreshing Spanish cold soup as a perfect dish for warm days!
