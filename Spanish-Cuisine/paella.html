Paella

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (400 g) Bomba or Arborio rice
  • 4 cups (1 L) chicken or seafood broth
  • 200 g chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 200 g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 100 g chorizo, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tsp (5 g) smoked paprika
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1 cup (240 ml) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley and lemon wedges for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large paella pan over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  • Step 2: In the same pan, cook the chorizo until it releases its oils, then add the onion, garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté until softened.
  • Step 3: Stir in the diced tomatoes, smoked paprika, and saffron. Let it simmer for 5 minutes to develop the flavors.
  • Step 4: Add the rice and mix well to coat in the spices. Pour in the broth and bring to a gentle simmer.
  • Step 5: Arrange the chicken and shrimp over the rice, then let it cook without stirring for about 20 minutes until the liquid is absorbed.
  • Step 6: Once the rice is tender and a crust (socarrat) forms on the bottom, remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges before serving. Enjoy this classic Spanish dish!
