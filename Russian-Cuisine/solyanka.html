Solyanka

Ingredients:

  • 500 g assorted meats (smoked sausage, ham, beef, or pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 pickles, diced
  • 1 can (400 g) diced tomatoes
  • 1.5 L beef broth
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) lemon juice
  • Fresh dill and sour cream for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot. Sauté the chopped onion until translucent and fragrant.
  • Step 2: Add the diced pickles, stirring to combine. Let them cook for about 3 minutes to release their juices.
  • Step 3: Stir in the diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 4: Pour in the beef broth and add the bay leaf. Let the mixture simmer for about 15 minutes.
  • Step 5: Add the assorted meats, season with paprika, salt, and black pepper, and stir well.
  • Step 6: Continue simmering for another 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  • Step 7: Stir in the lemon juice just before serving to add a tangy flavor.
  • Step 8: Serve hot, garnished with fresh dill and a dollop of sour cream.
