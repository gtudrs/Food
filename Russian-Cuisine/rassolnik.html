Rassolnik

Ingredients:

  • 500 g beef or pork ribs
  • 1.5 L beef broth
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 pickles, diced
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) pearl barley
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) pickle brine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp (5 g) black peppercorns
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large pot, bring the beef broth to a boil and add the beef or pork ribs. Simmer for 45 minutes to extract the flavor.
  • Step 2: Add the pearl barley to the broth and continue cooking for another 30 minutes until tender.
  • Step 3: In a separate pan, sauté the onion, grated carrot, and diced pickles until softened.
  • Step 4: Add the sautéed vegetables to the pot along with the diced potatoes, bay leaf, and peppercorns.
  • Step 5: Pour in the pickle brine and season with salt to taste. Simmer for another 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  • Step 6: Remove the bay leaf before serving and garnish with fresh dill.
  • Step 7: Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream if desired.
