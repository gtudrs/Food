Farcellets de col

Ingredients:

  • 8 large cabbage leaves
  • 300 g minced pork or beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp (5 g) paprika
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 cup (240 ml) tomato sauce

Steps:

  • Step 1: Bring a large pot of water to a boil and blanch the cabbage leaves for 2 minutes until soft. Drain and set aside.
  • Step 2: In a bowl, mix the minced meat, cooked rice, onion, garlic, egg, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Stir until well combined.
  • Step 3: Place a spoonful of the meat mixture onto each cabbage leaf, roll tightly, and secure with toothpicks if needed.
  • Step 4: Heat olive oil in a pan and lightly brown the cabbage rolls on all sides.
  • Step 5: Pour in the tomato sauce and let the rolls simmer for about 25-30 minutes until fully cooked.
  • Step 6: Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley and a side of crusty bread.
