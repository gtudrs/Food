Pad See Ew

Ingredients:

  • 200 g wide rice noodles
  • 200 g chicken or beef, sliced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp (5 g) sugar
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) white pepper
  • 1 cup (240 ml) Chinese broccoli, chopped
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  • Step 1: Soak the rice noodles in warm water until softened, then drain and set aside.
  • Step 2: Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken or beef and stir-fry until fully cooked, then set aside.
  • Step 3: In the same pan, add the remaining oil and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  • Step 4: Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them lightly before mixing them with the garlic.
  • Step 5: Add the softened noodles, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, sugar, and white pepper. Stir well to combine.
  • Step 6: Toss in the cooked meat and Chinese broccoli, stir-frying for another minute until the vegetables are tender.
  • Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy this delicious Thai stir-fried noodle dish!
