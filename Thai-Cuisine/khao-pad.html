Khao Pad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (400 g) cooked jasmine rice (preferably day-old)
  • 200 g chicken, shrimp, or tofu
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp (45 ml) vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) diced carrots and peas
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) fish sauce
  • 1 tsp (5 g) sugar
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) white pepper
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.
  • Step 2: Add the protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) and cook until fully done, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Step 3: Push everything to one side of the pan, add the eggs, and scramble until just set. Then mix with the protein.
  • Step 4: Stir in the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps, and mix well with the other ingredients.
  • Step 5: Add the diced carrots, peas, soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and white pepper. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes.
  • Step 6: Toss in the chopped green onions, stir briefly, and remove from heat.
  • Step 7: Serve hot with lime wedges on the side. Enjoy this classic Thai fried rice dish!
