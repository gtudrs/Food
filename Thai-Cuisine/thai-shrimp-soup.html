Thai Shrimp Soup

Ingredients:

  • 400 g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups (1 L) chicken broth
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised and chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) lime juice
  • 1 tsp (5 g) sugar
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) red curry paste
  • 1 cup (240 ml) mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 red chili, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a simmer. Add the lemongrass and garlic, allowing the flavors to infuse for about 5 minutes.
  • Step 2: Stir in the coconut milk, fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, and red curry paste. Mix well and bring to a gentle simmer.
  • Step 3: Add the sliced mushrooms and red chili, letting them cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  • Step 4: Gently add the shrimp and cook for about 3-5 minutes until pink and opaque.
  • Step 5: Taste and adjust seasoning if needed, adding more lime juice or fish sauce as desired.
  • Step 6: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor.
