Ribollita

Ingredients:

  • 400 g canned cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) dried rosemary
  • 1 liter vegetable broth
  • 200 g kale, chopped
  • 4 slices stale bread, torn into chunks
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions, carrots, and celery, cooking for about 5 minutes until softened.
  • Step 2: Stir in the minced garlic, thyme, and rosemary. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth, stirring well. Let the soup simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Step 4: Stir in the cannellini beans and chopped kale. Let everything cook together for another 10 minutes.
  • Step 5: Add the torn bread chunks to the soup and stir gently, allowing them to absorb the broth.
  • Step 6: Let the soup simmer for another 5 minutes. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  • Step 7: Serve warm, drizzled with extra olive oil for added flavor.
