Matar Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 250 g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup (150 g) green peas
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400 g) tomato puree
  • 1 tsp (5 g) garam masala
  • 1 tsp (5 g) cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp (5 g) coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they turn soft and golden.
  • Step 2: Stir in the garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the tomato puree, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and coriander powder. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.
  • Step 4: Add the green peas and cubed paneer, mixing gently to coat them in the sauce.
  • Step 5: Pour in the cream and mix well, letting it cook for another 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  • Step 6: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  • Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, alongside warm naan or steamed rice.
