Chickpea Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (800 g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400 g) tomato puree
  • 1 tsp (5 g) garam masala
  • 1 tsp (5 g) ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp (5 g) chili powder
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté for 3-4 minutes until they soften and turn golden. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  • Step 2: Add the drained chickpeas to the pan and stir well to coat them with the onion mixture. Let them cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing them to absorb some of the flavors.
  • Step 3: Stir in the tomato puree, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and chili powder. Mix thoroughly, ensuring the chickpeas are evenly coated in the flavorful sauce. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  • Step 4: Reduce the heat and pour in the coconut milk. Stir gently until the sauce becomes creamy and well-blended.
  • Step 5: Allow the mixture to simmer for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking and ensure the flavors fully develop.
  • Step 6: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  • Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, alongside rice or warm naan bread.
