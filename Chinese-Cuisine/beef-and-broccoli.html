Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450 g) flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp (5 g) ginger, grated
  • 2 cups (200 g) broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) beef broth
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a bowl, mix the sliced flank steak with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Stir well to coat the beef evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes to absorb the flavors and tenderize the meat.
  • Step 2: Heat a wok or large pan over medium-high heat. Add 1 tbsp of vegetable oil and swirl it around. Add the marinated beef and stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until browned. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  • Step 3: In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tbsp of vegetable oil. Sauté the garlic and grated ginger for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Step 4: Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2 minutes. Pour in the beef broth and let the broccoli steam for another 2-3 minutes until it becomes tender but still slightly crisp.
  • Step 5: Return the cooked beef to the pan and add the hoisin sauce, sesame oil, and black pepper. Stir well to coat everything evenly with the sauce.
  • Step 6: Cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly and coat the beef and broccoli perfectly.
  • Step 7: Serve hot over steamed rice, garnished with sesame seeds or sliced green onions for extra flavor.
