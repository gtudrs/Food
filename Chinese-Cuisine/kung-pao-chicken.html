Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500 g chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 3 dried red chilies
  • 50 g roasted peanuts
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sichuan peppercorns (crushed)
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps:

  • Step 1: Mix diced chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch in a bowl. Let it marinate for 15 minutes to absorb flavor and tenderize.
  • Step 2: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add marinated chicken and stir-fry for about 5 minutes until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  • Step 3: In the same pan, add minced garlic, ginger, and dried red chilies. Stir-fry for about 1 minute until aromatic.
  • Step 4: Add diced bell peppers and roasted peanuts. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes to slightly soften the vegetables while maintaining crunch.
  • Step 5: Return the cooked chicken to the pan. Stir in hoisin sauce, sesame oil, and Sichuan peppercorns. Mix well so everything is evenly coated.
  • Step 6: Let everything cook together for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  • Step 7: Garnish with freshly chopped spring onions and serve immediately with steamed rice.
