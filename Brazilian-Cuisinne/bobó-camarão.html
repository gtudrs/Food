Bobó de Camarão

Ingredients:

For the Shrimp Marinade:

  • 600 g fresh shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) lime juice
  • 1 tsp (5 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) paprika
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

For the Bobó (Stew Base):

  • 500 g cassava (yucca), peeled and diced
  • 1 L water or fish broth
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp (5 g) smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) chili flakes (optional, for spice)
  • 200 ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) dendê oil (palm oil, for authenticity)
  • Fresh coriander or parsley (for garnish)

Steps:

  • Step 1: Marinate the Shrimp: In a bowl, combine shrimp, lime juice, salt, black pepper, paprika, and minced garlic. Let it marinate for 20-30 minutes in the fridge.
  • Step 2: Cook the Cassava (Yucca): In a large pot, add cassava and 1 L of water or fish broth. Bring to a boil and cook for 20-25 minutes until soft. Drain and blend into a puree with half of the coconut milk. Set aside.
  • Step 3: Prepare the Stew Base: In the same pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté onion for 3 minutes until soft. Add garlic, bell pepper, tomato, smoked paprika, and chili flakes. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 4: Combine & Simmer: Stir in the cassava puree, remaining coconut milk, and dendê oil. Mix well and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes until thick and creamy.
  • Step 5: Cook the Shrimp: Add the marinated shrimp to the pot. Cook for 5-7 minutes, until shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  • Step 6: Garnish & Serve: Sprinkle with fresh coriander or parsley. Serve hot with steamed white rice and lime wedges.
