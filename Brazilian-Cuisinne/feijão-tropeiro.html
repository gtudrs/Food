Feijão Tropeiro

Ingredients:

Main Ingredients:

  • 500 g cooked carioca beans (or black beans, drained and rinsed)
  • 200 g smoked sausage (linguiça or chorizo), diced
  • 150 g bacon, diced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp (2 g) smoked paprika (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 g manioc flour (farofa) or cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) butter/li>
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley or spring onions (for garnish)

Optional Additions:

  • Kale or collard greens, finely sliced (for a traditional touch)
  • Crushed pork rinds (torresmo) for extra crunch

Steps:

  • Step 1: Cook the Beans (Skip if using canned beans): If using dried beans, soak them overnight. Cook in 1.5 L water with a bay leaf and salt until tender (about 35-40 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  • Step 2: Cook the Meats: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add bacon and cook until crispy (about 5 minutes). Add sausage and cook for 5 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 3: Sauté the Aromatics: Add onions and cook for 3 minutes until soft. Stir in garlic, black pepper, and smoked paprika.
  • Step 4: Scramble the Eggs: Push everything to one side of the pan. Crack 4 eggs into the empty side and scramble them. Once cooked, mix with the meats.
  • Step 5: Add Beans & Farofa: Stir in the cooked beans, mix well. Add manioc flour (farofa) and butter, tossing until combined.
  • Step 6: Serve & Garnish: Sprinkle with fresh parsley or chopped spring onions. Serve hot with rice, collard greens, and pork rinds (optional).
