Donburi

Ingredients:

  • 200 g cooked rice
  • 150 g thinly sliced beef or chicken
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) mirin
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) sake
  • 1 tsp (5 g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) dashi broth
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • Green onions for garnish
  • Pickled ginger for serving

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat a pan over medium heat and add the dashi broth, soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Stir to combine.
  • Step 2: Add the thinly sliced onions and let them simmer until they soften, about 5 minutes.
  • Step 3: Add the sliced beef or chicken, cooking until tender and fully cooked.
  • Step 4: Slowly drizzle the beaten egg over the mixture, covering it with a lid for 1-2 minutes until the egg is just set.
  • Step 5: Serve the mixture over a bowl of hot cooked rice.
  • Step 6: Garnish with green onions and pickled ginger.
  • Step 7: Enjoy your warm and comforting Donburi!
