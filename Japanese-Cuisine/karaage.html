Karaage

Ingredients:

  • 500 g boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) sake
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) mirin
  • 1 tsp (5 g) grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) potato starch
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a bowl, combine soy sauce, sake, mirin, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: In a separate bowl, mix the potato starch and flour. Coat each piece of marinated chicken evenly with the mixture.
  • Step 3: Heat the vegetable oil in a deep pan to 175°C (350°F). Fry the chicken in batches for about 5 minutes until golden and crispy.
  • Step 4: Remove the chicken and let it rest on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
  • Step 5: For extra crispiness, fry the chicken a second time for 1-2 minutes.
  • Step 6: Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and serve hot with lemon wedges.
  • Step 7: Enjoy your crispy and flavorful Karaage with rice or as a snack!
