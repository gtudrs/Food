Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 12 rice paper wrappers
  • 200 g cooked shrimp, sliced in half
  • 100 g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) fresh Thai basil
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) crushed peanuts (for garnish)
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) hoisin or peanut dipping sauce

Steps:

  • Step 1: Cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and rinse under cold water.
  • Step 2: Prepare the fresh vegetables and herbs by julienning the cucumber and carrot and setting them aside.
  • Step 3: Fill a large shallow dish with warm water. Dip each rice paper wrapper into the water for 5-10 seconds until softened.
  • Step 4: Lay the softened wrapper flat and arrange a few shrimp halves, rice noodles, julienned vegetables, and herbs in the center.
  • Step 5: Fold the sides of the wrapper inward, then tightly roll it up from the bottom to secure the filling.
  • Step 6: Repeat with the remaining ingredients to create 12 spring rolls.
  • Step 7: Serve fresh, garnished with crushed peanuts and accompanied by hoisin or peanut dipping sauce.
