Bo Luc Lac

Ingredients:

  • 400 g beef sirloin, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp (5 g) black pepper
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 200 g cooked rice
  • Fresh lettuce, tomato slices, and lime wedges for serving

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a bowl, mix the beef cubes with soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, minced garlic, and black pepper. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over high heat. Add the marinated beef and sear until browned on all sides.
  • Step 3: Toss in the sliced bell pepper and onion, stirring for another 2-3 minutes until slightly softened but still crisp.
  • Step 4: Remove from heat and serve the beef over a bed of fresh lettuce and tomato slices.
  • Step 5: Accompany with steamed rice and a squeeze of fresh lime juice for added flavor.
  • Step 6: Enjoy this classic Vietnamese shaking beef dish with its rich, savory, and slightly sweet flavors!
