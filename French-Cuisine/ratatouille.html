Ratatouille

Ingredients:

  • 1 eggplant, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups (500 ml) tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 1 tsp (5 g) dried thyme
  • 1 tsp (5 g) dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Prepare the vegetables by thinly slicing the eggplant, zucchini, and yellow squash. Dice the bell peppers and chop the onion finely.
  • Step 2: In a pan over medium heat, add olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until soft and fragrant. Then, add the diced bell peppers and cook for another 5 minutes until they become tender.
  • Step 3: Pour in the tomato sauce and season with thyme, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let it simmer for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  • Step 4: Spread a thin layer of the tomato mixture at the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange the sliced vegetables in a circular, overlapping pattern on top, alternating between eggplant, zucchini, and yellow squash.
  • Step 5: Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the vegetables and sprinkle additional thyme and basil for extra aroma.
  • Step 6: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.
  • Step 7: Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy warm, garnished with fresh basil if desired.
