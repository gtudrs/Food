Coq au Vin

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken legs and thighs
  • 1/2 bottle (375 ml) red wine
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 8 button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup (240 ml) chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp (15 g) tomato paste
  • 1 tsp (5 g) thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  • Step 1: In a large bowl, marinate the chicken in red wine with thyme, salt, and pepper for at least 4 hours or overnight to deepen the flavors.
  • Step 2: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped bacon and cook until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  • Step 3: In the same pan, brown the marinated chicken on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  • Step 4: Add onions, garlic, carrots, and mushrooms to the pan. Sauté for 5 minutes until softened.
  • Step 5: Stir in the tomato paste, then pour in the wine marinade and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer.
  • Step 6: Return the chicken and bacon to the pan. Cover and cook on low heat for 45 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend and the chicken to become tender.
  • Step 7: Serve hot with crusty bread or mashed potatoes for a rich and traditional French experience.
